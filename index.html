Understanding the 2025 LA Fires

In early 2025, Los Angeles County faced one of its most devastating wildfire seasons. Fueled by strong Santa Ana winds and dry conditions, the fires spread rapidly, impacting thousands of lives. This site provides an overview of key facts, recovery efforts, and resources to stay informed.

Multiple Major Fires: In January 2025, Los Angeles County experienced several significant wildfires, notably the Palisades and Eaton fires.

Destruction of Structures: The Palisades Fire destroyed approximately 6,837 structures and damaged 1,017 in Pacific Palisades and Malibu.

Human Casualties: The Palisades Fire led to 12 confirmed fatalities and four injuries.

Santa Ana Winds: Both fires were exacerbated by extremely powerful Santa Ana winds.

Evacuations and Displacement: Tens of thousands of residents were displaced due to mandatory evacuations.

Federal Assistance and Recovery Efforts: FEMA extended the deadline for wildfire disaster assistance applications to March 31, 2025.
